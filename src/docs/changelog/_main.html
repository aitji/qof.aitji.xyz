QoF's ChangeLogs

The ChangeLogs document the evolution of the QoF addon across versions. Each update focuses on refining existing mechanics, improving consistency, and introducing small but meaningful enhancements that align with vanilla Minecraft gameplay.

QoF follows an iterative development approach. Updates are released progressively, with each version bringing adjustments to modules, bug fixes, and occasional new features. The goal is to maintain a stable and cohesive experience while continuously improving usability and performance.
