What is QoF?

wiki is currently under maintained. Developer Note (@aitji, @pickerth-12)

QoF is a Minecraft Bedrock[1] addon that introduces small, vanilla-friendly features designed to improve gameplay without changing its core feel. Each module can be configured through the in-game pack settings panel, allowing players to customize their experience.

Quality of Feature was created by two pseudonym Thai[2] developers:

  • @aitji[3] scripting, design, and planning
  • @pickerth-12[4] JSON, Molang, data-side implementation, and design decisions

Fun Facts

  1. Did you know QoF was originally named QoL, but the name was too generic and difficult for SEO. It was later rebranded to QoF in version v1.2.3
  2. Did you know QoF was initially written in JavaScript, but due to frequent type-related issues, the entire project was rewritten in TypeScript in pre-version v1.4.1, and fully releases in version v1.5.0
  3. Did you know QoF was never originally planned for public release. It started as a personal addon focused on maintaining a vanilla-friendly style while improving quality of life features
